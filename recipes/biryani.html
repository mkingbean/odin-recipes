Chicken Biryani

Description

A recipe for a garlicy chicken biryani.

Ingredients

  • Onions
  • Rice
  • Ginger paste
  • Garlic paste
  • Tomato
  • Coriander
  • Mint
  • Yoghurt
  • Chili powder
  • Garam masala
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Lemon juice
  • Chicken

Steps

  1. Thinly slice the onions
  2. Fry the onions until crispy
  3. Add the chicken
  4. Add the ginger and garlic paste, lemon juice, and seasoning
  5. Slice the tomato and add it to the pan
  6. Add yoghurt, chopped coriander and chopped mint
  7. Cover and cook for about 20 minutes
  8. Boil the rice for no more than 4 minutes
  9. Place half the rice in a pan, then the chicken, then the rest of the rice
  10. Mix it together
