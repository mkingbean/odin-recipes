Lasagna

Description

A delicious lasagne recipe. You can replace the meat with Quorn mince for a veggie version.

Ingredients

  • Lasagne sheets
  • Chopped tomatoes
  • Tomato puree
  • Onions
  • Aubergine
  • Courgettes
  • Garlic paste
  • Red wine
  • Flour
  • Butter
  • Milk
  • Crushed almonds
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Oregano
  • Vegetable stock pot
  • Chedder cheese
  • Paremsan cheese
  • Mozerella cheese
  • Egg

Steps

  1. Chop the vegetables
  2. Fry the onions until soft
  3. Add the rest of the vegetables, garlic, and seasoning
  4. Add the mince cook until browned
  5. Add the chopped tomatoes, tomato puree, red wine, and stock pot
  6. Cover and leave to simmer
  7. In another pan melt the butter
  8. Mix in the flour
  9. Add the milk, egg and some parmesan, and whisk
  10. Layer the ingredients in a dish in the order tomato sauce, pasta sheets, white sauce, pasta sheets, cheese, repeat
  11. Top with almonds and seasoning
  12. Bake for 30 minutes at 200 degrees
